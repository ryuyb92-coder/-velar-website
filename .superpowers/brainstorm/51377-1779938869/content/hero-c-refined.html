Design decisions — Direction C Refined
Lighting
7 depth layers. Ceiling light from above, floor bounce from below, car mass in right quadrant. Not flat black — graduated warm dark with intentional bright zones.
Vehicle
Implied, not drawn. Three panel lines (roofline, chrome strip, body line) plus rim light + floor reflection. Car is atmosphere, not illustration.
Logo
420px wide max. Showroom spotlight glow behind it. Logo is the hero object — not a nav item. Left-aligned for editorial asymmetry vs. car on right.
Orange
Used at 4 points only. Logo (inherent), left architectural accent line, bottom frame rule, ghost CTA border. Never filled. Never decorative.
